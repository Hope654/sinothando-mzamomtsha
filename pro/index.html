Mzamomtsha Primary School


HOME ABOUT

Mzamomtsha Primary School is located between Khayelistha Township and
Umfuleni Township in the Western Cape Province of the Republic of South Africa.
The school was initially created for primary school students from grade 1 to grade 7,
but due to the rapid population growth (births and migration) the school is also enrolling higher grades
. Students are allowed up to grade 12 - which is the metric year.of South Africa.
The schools headmaster, Mr. Simphiwe Khuze, joined in 2007.


Stakeholders
